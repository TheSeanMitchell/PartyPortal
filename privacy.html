Privacy

Short version: we don't want your data

Party Portal is a static website with no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. We don't run analytics, we don't set advertising cookies, and we don't collect or sell personal information. There's nothing to log in to.

What stays on your device

Your favorite channels are saved in your browser's local storage, on your device only. They never leave your browser and are never sent to us or anyone else. Clear your browser data (or un-star them) and they're gone.

Third-party content

Two things on the page come from elsewhere:

  • Video players are embedded from public streams using the privacy-focused youtube-nocookie domain, which avoids personalised-ad cookies until you interact with a video. Once you press play, YouTube's own terms and privacy policy apply to that playback.
  • News headlines link out to their original publishers. When you click a headline you leave Party Portal and land on that publisher's site, which has its own privacy policy.
  • Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts.

We don't control those third parties and aren't responsible for their data practices.

Offline & installation

If you install Party Portal or visit again, a service worker stores a copy of the app's files on your device so it loads fast and works offline. That cache lives only on your device and contains no personal data.

Changes

If this ever changes, we'll update this page. Questions? See Contact.
