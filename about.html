About Party Portal

Your one-stop party destination

Party Portal is a single screen that pulls the best of global party culture into one place: live festival streams, DJ sets, nightlife cams from cities that never sleep, and a real-time feed of party-culture news. Press play and you're dropped into the party — no account, no sign-up, no clutter.

What's inside

  • 49+ live channels — EDM festivals, club sets, retro MTV, rock blocks and 24/7 city cams.
  • Stations & filters — tune the wall to Festivals, Clubs, Retro, Live Cams and more, or fire up a curated mix like Peak Hours or Throwback.
  • Favorites — star the channels you love; they're saved to your device and one tap away.
  • Party Mode — a full-screen video wall of twelve channels at once (desktop).
  • Mobile player — one channel at a time, swipe or shuffle to the next.
  • Festival countdowns — live timers and ticket links to the next big drops.
  • Culture feed — real headlines from the music and nightlife press.

How the news works

The culture feed is 100% real. A small bot gathers headlines from trusted music and nightlife publications (Mixmag, DJ Mag, Resident Advisor, Billboard, Rolling Stone and more) via public news feeds, cleans and de-duplicates them, filters out anything off-topic or negative, and refreshes the feed three times a day. Every headline links straight to the original publisher. We don't write or invent stories.

How it's built

Party Portal is a fast, static web app — no framework, no server, no database. It runs as a single page hosted on GitHub Pages, with the news bot running on a schedule via GitHub Actions. Video is embedded from public streams using privacy-friendly youtube-nocookie players. It also installs as an app and works offline for the parts that don't need a live connection.

A note on the streams

Live feeds are embedded from publicly available streams owned by their respective broadcasters and creators. Availability can change at any time — if a channel goes dark, hit Shuffle for another, or open Party Mode for a fresh wall.
